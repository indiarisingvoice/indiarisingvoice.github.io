Quarterly Strategic Review

Q1 2026 | January–March

Executive Overview

Q1 2026 reflected structural recalibration rather than abrupt systemic shifts. Multipolar negotiation density continued to increase, financial diversification trends persisted, and technological competition deepened. India’s positioning remained calibrated and stability-oriented.

Global Structural Developments

Regional coalitions strengthened diplomatic density without forming rigid blocs. Negotiation platforms expanded, particularly within emerging power forums. Power redistribution remained incremental rather than disruptive.

Financial & Monetary Trends

Currency settlement experimentation continued across select trade corridors. Reserve diversification discussions persisted, though dollar liquidity dominance remained intact. Financial fragmentation remained evolutionary.

Energy & Infrastructure Signals

Energy security discourse increasingly emphasized supply chain resilience over simple access metrics. Maritime logistics stability remained critical. Renewable capacity acceleration continued across major economies.

Technology & AI Landscape

AI capability competition expanded through infrastructure investment rather than regulatory confrontation. Compute capacity, semiconductor access, and data governance frameworks remained central strategic variables.

India’s Strategic Position

India maintained calibrated multilateral engagement while preserving strategic autonomy. Energy diversification and digital infrastructure scaling reinforced macro stability. External positioning remained balanced across major power centers.

Risk Outlook – Q2 2026

  • Geopolitical escalation affecting energy corridors
  • Global liquidity tightening pressures
  • Technology supply bottlenecks
  • Policy execution risks in emerging markets

Strategic Implications

  • Institutional depth remains the decisive leverage multiplier.
  • Energy and financial resilience underpin geopolitical flexibility.
  • Optionality defines sustainable multipolar positioning.
